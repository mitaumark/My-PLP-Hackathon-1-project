About Me

I am a final year Medical Laboratory Science student with a passion for technology.
I have hands-on experience in advanced medical diagnostic laboratories, and I am skilled

and clinical chemistry. I am also a dedicated, curious, and enthusiastic tech guy, with expertise

learn new skills and take on new challenges. I believe that the intersection of medicine and technology holds great potential
for improving patient care and outcomes, and I am excited to be a part of this evolving field.
